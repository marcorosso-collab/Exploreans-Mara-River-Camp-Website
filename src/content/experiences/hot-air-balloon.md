The balloon lifts off before sunrise. As the light breaks over the escarpment, the Mara unfolds below — a patchwork of grassland, forest, and river bends dotted with grazing herds. From up here, the scale of the ecosystem becomes clear in a way no game drive can match.

The flight lasts roughly an hour, ending with a bush breakfast on the savanna. It is one of those experiences that stays with you long after you leave.
